## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

1. **Consistency Over Perfection**
   - Publish 10 blogs Month 1 (even if rough) beats waiting for perfect strategy
   - Small improvements compound monthly

2. **Focus on Conversion, Not Just Traffic**
   - 300 sessions at 5% conversion (15 leads) beats 1000 at 0.5% (5 leads)
   - Optimize CTA placement, form fields, WhatsApp integration

3. **Build Real Backlinks, Not Spammy**
   - Quality 5 backlinks from DA 50+ beats 100 from DA 10
   - Guest posts, partnerships, earned media > buying links

4. **Track Everything**
   - Daily: Blog publish status, featured snippet wins
   - Weekly: Ranking positions, traffic trend, lead count
   - Monthly: KPI dashboard update, strategy adjustment

5. **Don't Compete with JustDial on Scale**
   - Own the "Lok Adalat expert" space (they don't specialize)
   - Build brand loyalty (they're transactional)
   - Focus on conversion quality, not traffic volume
